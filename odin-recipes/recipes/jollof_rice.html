Jollof Rice

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup uncooked long-grain rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 Scotch bonnet peppers, chopped (optional)

Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, ginger, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook until onion is translucent.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they break down and the mixture thickens.
  4. Add rice to the pot, stirring to coat with the tomato mixture.
  5. Add water to the pot and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until rice is cooked.
